Coding Quiz Challenge


This quiz will challenge your knowledge of what we have learned over the past two weeks. There are a total of five multiple choice questions. You will be given a total time of 60 seconds to complete the quiz. However, if you miss an answer, you will lose 5 seconds. You can see the time remaining in the upper right corner of the screen. You cannot move on to the next question until you are correct. Your score will be shown at the end when you have completed the quiz.
